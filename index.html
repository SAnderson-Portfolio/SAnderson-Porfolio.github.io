Welcome

Hi! I’m Steven, a graduate programmer from Abertay University. I love programming, game development, and problem-solving. I’m ready and excited to take my first steps into the industry. I’m looking forward to collaborating, coding, and learning as much as possible.
My skill sets are already well developed;

  • Languages: C++, C#, Python, Java.
  • Version Control Software: Git.
  • Project Management Tools: Excel, Trello, Jira.
  • Software: Visual Studio, DirectX 11, Spyder, Android Studio, RenderDoc, SFML.
  • Engines: Unreal Engine, Unity, GameMaker.

I love game jam events and have participated in loads over the years. I have also been invited to be a mentor for a number of events. The collaborative creativity of these events and game development, more generally, is what really excites me. During my university course, I was given the opportunity to work for the Scottish Crannog Centre. This involved working as part of a team as a lead programmer of a group consisting of all disciplines. It was great to see that all team members could use the tools and systems I produced to streamline the development process to meet the client’s deadlines and expectations for the brief they provided.
