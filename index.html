Welcome

Hi! I’m Steven a graduate programmer from Abertay University. I love programming, game development and problem solving. I’m ready and excited to take my first steps into industry. I’m looking forward to collaborating, coding, and learning as much as possible.
My skill sets are already well developed;

  • Languages: C++, C#, Python, Java.
  • Version Control Software: Git.
  • Project Management Tools: Excel, Trello, Jira.
  • Software: Visual Studio, DirectX 11, Spyder, Android Studio, RenderDoc, SFML.
  • Engines: Unreal Engine, Unity, GameMaker.

I love game jam events, and have participated in loads over the years. I started out as a participant and have stayed on as a mentor to younger game developers more recently. The collaborative creativity of these events, and of game development more generally, is what really excites me. During my university course, I was given the opportunity to work for the Scottish Crannog Centre. This again involved working as part of a team but this time as a lead programmer of a team consisting of all disciplines, it was great to see that the tools and systems I produced could be used by all disciplines to streamline the development process. to meet the client’s deadlines and expectations for the brief they provided.
