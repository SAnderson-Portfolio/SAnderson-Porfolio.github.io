Hello!
I'm Steven

I'm a graduate programmer and love learning

I enjoy coding and game development with a passion, and the problem solving that comes along with it. As a graduate, I know that I still have a lot to learn about the industry and programming but I am keen to learn it.

Over the years I have learnt a bit though:
  • Languages: C++, C#, Python, Java.
  • Version Control Software: Git.
  • Project Management Tools: Excel, Trello, Jira.
  • Software: Visual Studio, DirectX 11, Spyder, Android Studio, RenderDoc, SFML.
  • Engines: Unreal Engine, Unity, GameMaker.

I love working in teams and keeping busy

Throughout my education, I have had the opportunity to be involved in various game jam events as a participant or a mentor. I enjoy the creative process involved in these events because not only does it test my skills but having others to challenge your decisions is invaluable.

During my university course, I was given the opportunity to work for a client, this involved working as part of a team as lead programmer to meet the client’s deadlines and expectations for the brief they provided.
